Who am I?

I am Rajib Jadav, a self-taught UI/UX Designer and growth-stage Frontend Developer based in Kolkata, India. With 3+ years of experience, I have designed and contributed to 20+ e-commerce platforms, SaaS dashboards, and web/mobile products across B2C, B2B, and D2C domains.

I believe great digital products are built at the intersection of human psychology, visual design, and solid coding fundamentals. Instead of relying on degrees, I focus on curiosity, execution, and continuous learning—using AI tools as productivity support while deeply understanding how and why design and code decisions work.
